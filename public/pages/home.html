Why Educationist Tutoring?

Free Classes

All tutors part of Educationist Tutoring are volunteers, meaning they are helping out of the good in their hearts. No payments are taken in exchange for classes and you can request as many different subjects and classes you need help with

Flexible Timings

Once matched with a tutor and they reach out to you through email, you will be able to communicate and decide a time that works best for both of you. If you need to reschedule or cancel, you can easily let your tutor know.

Personalized

All Educationist Classes are 1-on-1 meaning your student can get specialized assistance based on their needs. Cuppled with our vast variety of content from tutors and curators, we have material for all students!

Our Dashboard

Easily request and find students

You can request a class if you are a student, or find classes in the subjects you are accepted in and get matched.
Create and find content

On top of being a tutor, you can upload content you made for classes for other tutors and get volunteering hours
Log Hours & Track Class Progress

On the classes tab you can log your hours after a class, see total hours, and see a graph of your progress.
Get Certificates

To verify your hours for NHS, college applications, etc, you can request a certificate on the logs page and get it in 48 hours
