The Timeline

Step 1Start now!

Make an account on the dashboard!

On the dashboard you will be able to manage everything related to Educationist! This includes creating new requests in classes, exploring content, and managing classes with your tutors.

Making an account
Step 2

Create a request

On the requests page, fill in the information for your specific request and submit it in the dashboard. From there, all our tutors will be able to see your request.

Requesting
Step 31-2 weeks

Wait to be matched

After you submit a request, all the accepted tutors will see it on the dashboard and will match with you. This process can take a bit around 1-2 weeks

Invoice Graphs
Step 4

Start Meeting

Once you are matched with a tutor and you reach out to each other, decide a data and time and start meeting on a weekly basis
